– Hello

I'm Dipu Rai

Data Editor & Investigative Journalist


I'm a data journalist and editor based in New Delhi, specialising in data-driven storytelling across finance, health, politics, and environment. I currently serve as Data Editor of India Today's Data Intelligence Unit (DIU) and Course Director for the Data Journalism Certificate Program at India Today Media Institute (ITMI).

With over two decades of experience across broadcast, print, and digital platforms, I combine strong analytical skills with storytelling expertise to produce publication-ready visualisations and investigative reports. My career spans major Indian media organisations including Network 18, Zee Media, and DNA Newspaper, where I covered the Finance Ministry and produced groundbreaking investigative stories.

I'm well-versed in Python, R, SQL and basic Java Script, especially TypeScript, and can produce both prototype and publication-ready visualisations. I specialise in handling a variety of datasets—parsing, scraping, cleaning and statstical analysis—and have particular expertise in spatial analysis using tools like QGIS, Carto, and Google Earth Engine.

My work has sparked several financial investigations and earned recognition including the prestigious Redink Award for financial crime reporting in 2015. I recently completed an advanced course in building and evaluating AI newsroom workflows at Columbia Journalism School with Jonathan Soma.

After completing my master's in economics, I started my career as a business journalist. I've also translated Raymond Williams's book 'Television' into Hindi. In my spare time, I enjoy reading code, learning new visualisation techniques, reading books, listening to music, and watching films.
